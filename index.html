Powerful

heRo apps are powered by R, the open-source statistical computing language. With access to the thousands of available R packages, heRo apps can perform the full spectrum of analytics required by HEOR and market access proffesionals.

Interactive


heRo apps feature beautiful interactive data visualizations designed to answer your research questions in real-time. Our specially-designed user interface allows you to run what-if analyses on any parameter and immediately see the effect on any output.

Anywhere, Anytime


With heRo, your work is saved in the cloud and accessible to you from any internet-connected device. You can craft your analysis from your workstation and take it with you on your tablet.

Secure


Our apps come with secure access control and an administrative panel which allows you to grant/revoke access, delegate administrative responsbilities, and set security policies.


heRo is PAI's web-based platform for deploying interactive HEOR applications. Whether you need to find the value-based price of your new treatment, run a network meta-anlaysis to assess the competitive landscape, or simply perform exploratory data analysis, we can build an application to meet your needs and your budget.

PAI (Policy Analysis Inc.) is a 40-year old health-economics consultancy located in Brookline, Massachusetts. We are an industry leader in the fields of pharmacoeconomics, outcomes research, quality-of-life assessment, pharmacoepidemiology, and health technology assessment.

Our consultants have advanced training in a variety of fields, including health economics, medicine, management science, epidemiology, biostatistics, health-services research, and survey design, and have authored more than 250 articles in major peer-reviewed journals.
